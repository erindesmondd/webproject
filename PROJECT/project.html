The Rising of Gymnastics Champions

Gymnastics comes from a greek word meaning disciplinary exercises. It challenges individuals to exercise self-control, coordination, flexibility, strength and gracefulness. In 1774, gymnastics transitioned from greek tumbling to a more modern version which we see today. There are so many gymnasts in the past that we remember today adn continue to be inspired by. Nadia Comaneci has been the gymnast that I have looked up to for years. She strived for perfection is everything she did and left an everlasting impact on the sport of gymnastics. Through the generations of people, we have seen more and more success from young and upcoming gymnasts, thanks to those that have done it in the past.
