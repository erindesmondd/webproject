Nastia Liukin (1989- )

"If you are afraid of failure, you dont deserve success." -Nastia Liukin

Nastia Liukin, 2008 Olympic All Around Champion has inspired so many with her incredible accompishments. In 2005 and 2007, Nastia took home the world beam title and in 2005 the world bar title. Her coach was her father, Valeri Liukin, who continues to coach the United States National Team today. Nastia continues to inspire young gymnasts to follow their dreams and never give up no matter what happpen s.
