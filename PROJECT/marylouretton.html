Mary Lou Retton (1990- )

"Each of us has a fire in our hearts for something. It's our goal in life to find it and keep it lit." -Mary Lou Retton

Mary Lou Retton is a former American All Around gymnast. In 1984, at the Summer Olympics, Mary took home the individual all around gold medal in Los Angeles. She also took home two silver medals and two bronze medals along with her all around title. Alomg with taking home numerous medals from the olympics, she also came away with two perfect 10.0s. She is a favorite of many in the gymnastics world and still continues to to touch the lives and inspire many generations of young athletes.
