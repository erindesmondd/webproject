Kim Zmeskal (1976- )

"I've learned that you don't have to win first place to win." -Kim Zmeskal

Kim Zmeskal was a national champion, a world champion, and an Olympic bronze medalist. She was coached by Marta Karolyi alongside her husband Bela Karolyi. She currently coaches at Texas Dreams and has been U.S. National Team Member, Ragan Smith's coach for years. She hopes to be able to continue her gymnastics career through her current gymnasts.
